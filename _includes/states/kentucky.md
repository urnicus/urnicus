**Voter List Access**

[Request Voter Registration Data](https://elect.ky.gov/Candidates/Pages/Request-Voter-Registration-Data.aspx)

[Request Form](https://elect.ky.gov/Candidates/Documents/SBE%2084%20%20Request%20for%20Voter%20Registration%20Data%2007-2022%20-%20FILLABLE.pdf)

**Voter History**

[Request For Voter Registration Data Pricing (PDF)](https://elect.ky.gov/Candidates/Documents/Request%20for%20Voter%20Registration%20Data%20Pricing%20and%20Report%20Formatting.pdf)

Five year voting history included in the file. Indicates if they voted in the primary and if they voted in the general that year. County and Municipal elections are synchronized with Federal elections. Kentucky is one of only five states that holds elections for its highest state offices in odd-numbered years.

**Cost**

$4,000
