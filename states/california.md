# California

**Untested | Access: Mail in | Expense: $**

**Voter List Access**

[Votecal Project](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voter-registration/votecal-project)

[Voter File Application (PDF)](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/pdfs/voter-file-application.pdf)

Mail in form - must be a qualified applicant for approval

Qualified Applicant requirements listed on the form:
- Political
- Campaign/Committee
- Academic
- Media
- Private Vendor
- Legal
- Governmental
- Investigation
- Other (specify)

**Voter History**

Included with the voter list

**Cost**

$100
