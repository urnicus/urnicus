Minnesota provides access to voter registration data and voter history at an exceptionally affordable price of $46. However, access is restricted to registered Minnesota voters, and the data may only be used for purposes related to elections, political activities, or law enforcement.

## Quick Summary

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Statewide Cost** | $46 |
| **Voter List Access** | Mail-in request form |
| **Voter History Access** | Included with voter list |
| **Delivery Method** | Contact SOS for delivery details |
| **Processing Time** | After form and payment received |
| **Eligibility** | Must be registered Minnesota voter |

## Eligibility Requirements

> **Important Requirement:**
>
> This information is only available to **registered Minnesota voters**, and may only be used for purposes related to elections, political activities, or law enforcement.
>
> Source: Minnesota Statutes 201.091

### Permitted Uses
- Elections-related activities
- Political activities
- Law enforcement purposes

## Voter List

Minnesota provides voter registration data through the Secretary of State's office to qualified requestors.

### How to Access

1. Visit the [Minnesota SOS Registered Voter List Requests page](https://www.sos.mn.gov/election-administration-campaigns/data-maps/registered-voter-list-requests/)
2. Download the [Registered Voter List Request Form PDF](https://www.sos.mn.gov/media/2641/registered-voter-list-request-form.pdf)
3. Complete the form (must affirm you are a registered Minnesota voter)
4. Mail the form with payment ($46) to the Secretary of State
5. Receive voter data after processing

### What's Included

The voter registration file includes information for registered voters in Minnesota.

## Voter History

Minnesota includes voter history data with the voter registration list.

### Historical Coverage

Voter history records are provided along with the voter registration data. Contact the Secretary of State for details about the historical coverage period.

## Cost

Minnesota charges just **$46** for statewide voter registration data with voter history, making it one of the most affordable states for comprehensive voter data.

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

> **Legal Restrictions:**
>
> According to Minnesota Statutes 201.091:
> - Access is limited to registered Minnesota voters
> - Data may only be used for elections, political activities, or law enforcement purposes
> - Commercial use is prohibited

When requesting Minnesota voter data, requestors must affirm their registration status and intended use complies with state law.

## Technical Notes

### Registration Verification

The request form requires you to affirm that you are a registered Minnesota voter. The Secretary of State may verify registration status before processing requests.

### Delivery Method

Contact the Secretary of State's office or review the request form for information about how data is delivered after approval.

### Testing Status

This system has not been independently tested. Contact the Minnesota Secretary of State for current information about:
- Processing timelines
- Delivery methods
- File formats
- Registration verification process
