Ohio provides completely free access to its statewide voter registration data and voter history through an online portal. The state offers one of the most accessible voter data systems in the United States with immediate download capability and no cost barriers.

## Voter List

Ohio provides voter registration data through the Secretary of State's online FTP portal, making it accessible to anyone at no cost.

### How to Access

1. Visit the [Ohio Secretary of State Voter FTP Portal](https://www6.ohiosos.gov/ords/f?p=VOTERFTP:STWD:::#stwdVtrFiles)
2. Select the statewide voter files you need
3. Download immediately from the portal

### What's Included

The voter file includes comprehensive voter registration information for all registered voters in Ohio.

## Voter History

Ohio includes voter history data directly in the voter list file, eliminating the need for a separate request.

### Historical Coverage

Voter history records are included in the downloadable file, providing participation records for registered voters.

## Cost

Ohio's voter data is **completely free** to access and download, making it one of the most accessible states for obtaining comprehensive voter information.

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

When accessing Ohio voter data, users should be aware of applicable state laws regarding the use of voter registration records. Check the portal for current terms of use and legal requirements.

## Technical Notes

### File Format

Files are provided through an FTP-style download portal with immediate access to current data.

### Testing Status

This system has been tested and verified to work correctly. Downloads are immediate and the process is straightforward.
