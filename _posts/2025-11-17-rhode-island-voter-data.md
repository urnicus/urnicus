## Voter List

Rhode Island provides voter registration data through the Secretary of State's office with multiple delivery format options.

### How to Access

1. Download the [Voter List Order Form PDF](https://vote.sos.ri.gov/forms/elections/forms/voter-list-orderform.pdf)
2. Complete the order form
3. Select your preferred delivery format (email recommended for cost and updates)
4. Mail the form with payment to the address specified
5. Receive voter data in your selected format

### What's Included

The voter registration file includes information for registered voters in Rhode Island.

## Voter History

The availability of voter history data is not explicitly stated on the order form.

### Recommendation

When submitting your order form, specifically inquire about:
- Whether voter history is available
- What historical coverage is provided
- Whether it's included or requires an additional fee
- File format for voter history data

## Cost

Rhode Island offers multiple pricing options based on delivery format:

| Delivery Format | Cost | Updates Included |
|----------------|------|------------------|
| **Email** | **$25** | **3 free updates** |
| Other formats | Varies | Check order form |

The email delivery option at $25 with three free updates offers the best value.

## Free Updates

When you purchase voter data via email delivery, Rhode Island includes **three free updates**, ensuring you have access to current voter registration information as it changes throughout the year.

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

When requesting Rhode Island voter data, users must comply with state laws regarding the use of voter registration records. The order form will include specific terms of use and legal requirements.

## Technical Notes

The email delivery option provides several benefits:
- Lowest cost ($25)
- Three free updates included
- Immediate delivery (faster than physical media)
- No shipping delays or costs
- Easy to archive and manage digital files

### Delivery Format Options

Review the order form for all available delivery formats. While email is recommended for most users, other options may be available for specific needs.
