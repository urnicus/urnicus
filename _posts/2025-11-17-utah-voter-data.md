Utah provides access to voter registration data through the Lieutenant Governor's office for $1,050. The state includes voter history covering the last two election cycles with the voter registration list.

## Quick Summary

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Statewide Cost** | $1,050 |
| **Voter List Access** | Contact Lieutenant Governor's office |
| **Voter History Access** | Included (last two election cycles) |
| **Delivery Method** | Contact office for delivery details |
| **Processing Time** | After request and payment |

## Voter List

Utah provides voter registration data through the Lieutenant Governor's Elections Office.

### How to Access

1. Visit the [Utah Elections Data page](https://vote.utah.gov/obtain-voter-registration-or-election-data/)
2. Review the information and requirements for obtaining voter data
3. Contact the Elections Office to request voter registration data
4. Complete any required forms or agreements
5. Submit payment ($1,050)
6. Receive voter data after processing

### What's Included

The voter registration file includes comprehensive information for all registered voters in Utah.

## Voter History

Utah includes voter history data with the voter registration list.

### Historical Coverage

Utah provides voter history for the **last two election cycles**.

This coverage includes participation records for recent elections, providing useful data for understanding voter engagement patterns.

## Cost

Utah charges **$1,050** for statewide voter registration data with voter history.

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

When requesting Utah voter data, users must comply with state laws regarding the use of voter registration records. The Elections Office will provide specific terms of use and legal requirements during the request process.

## Technical Notes

### Contact-Based System

Utah requires direct contact with the Lieutenant Governor's Elections Office rather than an automated online system. When contacting the office, be prepared to discuss:
- Your specific data needs
- Intended use for the data
- Delivery method preferences
- Payment arrangements
- File format requirements

### Two Election Cycles

Utah's voter history coverage of "last two election cycles" typically includes:
- Most recent general election
- Most recent primary election
- Previous general election
- Previous primary election

Confirm the specific elections included when making your request.

### Testing Status

This system has not been independently tested. Contact the Utah Elections Office for current information about:
- Request procedures
- Required forms or agreements
- Processing timelines
- Payment methods
- Delivery methods
- File formats
- Specific election cycles included in voter history
