Wisconsin provides access to voter registration data and voter history through the "Badger Voters" online portal. While the estimated cost of approximately $12,500 for a complete statewide list is significant, the portal system provides a modern, accessible interface for obtaining Wisconsin voter data.

## Voter List

Wisconsin provides voter registration data through the "Badger Voters" online portal, a creative naming choice that reflects the state's identity.

### How to Access

1. Visit [Badger Voters](https://badgervoters.wi.gov/)
2. Create an account or log in
3. Navigate through the portal to request voter data
4. Select statewide data or specific jurisdictions
5. Complete payment
6. Download voter data through the portal

### What's Included

The voter registration file includes comprehensive information for all registered voters in Wisconsin.

## Voter History

Wisconsin includes voter history data with the voter registration list available through the Badger Voters portal.

### Historical Coverage

Voter history records are provided along with the voter registration data. Check the portal or contact the Wisconsin Elections Commission for details about the historical coverage period.

## Cost

Wisconsin charges approximately **$12,500** for the complete statewide voter list with voter history.

This cost estimate should be confirmed through the Badger Voters portal, as pricing may vary based on:
- Specific data selections
- File format options
- Additional data fields requested

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

When accessing Wisconsin voter data through Badger Voters, users must comply with state laws regarding the use of voter registration records. The portal will include specific terms of use and legal requirements during the account creation and purchase process.

## Technical Notes

### Badger Voters Portal

Wisconsin's "Badger Voters" portal branding provides a memorable, state-specific identity for voter data access. The name references Wisconsin's nickname as "The Badger State."

The online portal system provides:
- Web-based account management
- Customizable data selections
- Secure payment processing
- Download capabilities
- Modern user interface

### Pricing Structure

The ~$12,500 estimated cost for statewide data is significantly higher than most states. This pricing structure likely reflects:
- System maintenance costs
- Data quality and accuracy
- Regular updates and support
- Portal infrastructure

Consider whether you need complete statewide data or if specific county or municipal data would meet your needs at a lower cost.
