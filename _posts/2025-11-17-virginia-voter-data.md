Virginia provides access to voter registration data and voter history at an exceptionally affordable rate of $9.50 per million records. However, access requires meeting eligibility requirements such as being a candidate, political party, or PAC, with a verification process to confirm qualification.

## Quick Summary

## Eligibility Requirements

Virginia restricts access to voter registration data to qualified requestors who meet specific criteria.

### Who Can Access

Eligible requestors include:
- **Candidates** for elective office
- **Political parties**
- **Political Action Committees (PACs)**
- Other qualified entities as defined by Virginia law

### Verification Process

Virginia conducts a verification process to confirm requestors meet eligibility requirements before providing access to voter data.

## Voter List

Virginia provides voter registration data through the Department of Elections' Client Services division.

### How to Access

1. Visit the [Virginia Client Services page](https://www.elections.virginia.gov/candidatepac-info/client-services/)
2. Review eligibility requirements
3. Fill out the request form (available on the page)
4. Submit the form for verification
5. Department of Elections will verify you meet eligibility criteria (Candidate, Party, PAC, etc.)
6. Upon approval, pay the fee ($9.50 per million records)
7. Receive voter registration data

### What's Included

The voter registration file includes information for registered voters in Virginia.

## Voter History

Virginia includes voter history data with the voter registration list when requested.

### How to Access

When submitting your request form, indicate that you want voter history included with the voter registration data.

### Historical Coverage

Contact the Department of Elections or review information provided during the request process for details about the historical coverage period included in the voter history file.

## Cost

Virginia offers some of the most affordable voter data in the United States:

**Pricing:** $9.50 per million records

| Records | Cost |
|---------|------|
| 1,000,000 | $9.50 |
| 2,000,000 | $19.00 |
| 3,000,000 | $28.50 |
| 4,000,000 | $38.00 |
| **~6,000,000 (statewide est.)** | **~$57.00** |

For Virginia's estimated 6 million registered voters, the total cost for statewide data with voter history would be approximately **$57**, making it one of the least expensive states for comprehensive voter information.
