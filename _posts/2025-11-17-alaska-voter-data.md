## Voter List

Alaska provides voter registration data through the Division of Elections, though the process requires direct contact rather than an online purchase system.

### How to Access

1. Visit the [Alaska Division of Elections Research page](https://www.elections.alaska.gov/research/#akcl)
2. Contact any divisional office for assistance:
   - Email: elections@alaska.gov
   - Phone: Contact information available on the research page
3. Request the voter registration list
4. Inquire about delivery method and timeline

### What's Included

The voter registration file includes information for registered voters in Alaska.

## Voter History

Based on available documentation, voter history appears to be included with the voter registration list purchase.

### Recommendation

When contacting the Division of Elections, specifically confirm:
- Whether voter history is included with the voter list
- What historical coverage is provided
- File format and delivery method

## Cost

According to Ballotpedia, Alaska charges approximately **$20** for voter registration data. This should be confirmed when contacting the Division of Elections.

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

When requesting Alaska voter data, users must comply with state laws regarding the use of voter registration records. The Division of Elections will provide specific terms of use and legal requirements during the request process.

## Technical Notes

### Contact-Based System

Alaska operates a contact-based request system rather than an automated online portal. Direct communication with the Division of Elections is required to:
- Confirm current pricing
- Specify data needs
- Arrange delivery method
- Clarify what's included

### Testing Status

This system has not been independently tested. Contact the Division of Elections for current information about the request process, pricing, and data availability.
