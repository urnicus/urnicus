Maine provides access to voter registration data and voter history for $2,200 through a formal request process. Access requires meeting specific eligibility criteria such as being a candidate, PAC, issue committee, or political party.

## Quick Summary

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Statewide Cost** | $2,200 |
| **Voter List Access** | Mail-in data request form |
| **Voter History Access** | Included with voter list |
| **Delivery Method** | Contact SOS for delivery details |
| **Processing Time** | After form review and payment |
| **Eligibility** | Must meet criteria (candidate, PAC, party, etc.) |

## Eligibility Requirements

Maine restricts access to voter data to qualified requestors who meet specific criteria:

- Candidates for office
- Political Action Committees (PACs)
- Issue committees
- Political parties
- Other qualified entities as defined by Maine law

Review the data request form for complete eligibility requirements.

## Voter List

Maine provides voter registration data from the Central Voter Registration (CVR) system through the Secretary of State's office.

### How to Access

1. Visit the [Maine Elections Voter Data page](https://www.maine.gov/sos/elections-voting/voter-data)
2. Download the [CVR Data Request Form PDF](https://www.maine.gov/sos/sites/maine.gov.sos/files/content/assets/MAR24-20-Rev.-203-21--20CVR-20Data-20Request-20Form.pdf)
3. Review eligibility requirements
4. Complete the data request form
5. Mail the form with payment ($2,200) to the address specified
6. Receive voter data after processing

### What's Included

The CVR data file includes comprehensive voter registration information for registered voters in Maine.

## Voter History

Maine includes voter history data with the voter registration list for qualified requestors.

### Historical Coverage

Voter history records are provided along with the voter registration data. Contact the Secretary of State for details about the historical coverage period.

## Cost

Maine charges **$2,200** for statewide voter registration data with voter history.

Source: [Fact Sheet on Obtaining Data from CVR (DOC file)](https://www.maine.gov/sos/sites/maine.gov.sos/files/content/assets/MAR24-20rev321-20Fact-20Sheet-20on-20Obtaining-20Data-20from-20CVR.doc)

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

> **Important Legal Requirements:**
>
> Access to Maine voter data is restricted to specific qualified entities including candidates, PACs, issue committees, and political parties.

When requesting Maine voter data, qualified requestors must comply with state laws regarding the use of voter registration records. The data request form will include specific terms of use and legal requirements.

## Technical Notes

### Qualification Review

Maine reviews data requests to ensure requestors meet eligibility criteria. Be prepared to provide:
- Documentation of your candidate status, PAC registration, or other qualifying criteria
- Intended use for the data
- Compliance with legal requirements

### Delivery Method

Contact the Secretary of State's office or review the data request form for information about how data is delivered after approval and payment.
