Oklahoma provides free access to voter registration data and voter history through an online portal for qualified requestors. While access requires meeting specific eligibility criteria, approved users can access comprehensive voter data at no cost.

## Quick Summary

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Statewide Cost** | Free |
| **Voter List Access** | Online portal after registration |
| **Voter History Access** | Included with voter list |
| **Delivery Method** | Online access via data warehouse |
| **Processing Time** | After account approval |
| **Eligibility** | Must meet qualification criteria |

## Eligibility Requirements

Access to Oklahoma's voter data is restricted to qualified requestors who meet specific criteria:

- Bona fide residents of Oklahoma who are U.S. citizens
- Official representatives of recognized political parties in Oklahoma
- Candidates for offices in Oklahoma and their official representatives
- Other persons, as authorized by state law

## Voter List

Oklahoma provides voter registration data through the Oklahoma Election Data Warehouse, an online portal system.

### How to Access

1. Review eligibility requirements on the [Oklahoma Elections voter list page](https://oklahoma.gov/elections/candidates/voter-registration-list.html)
2. Complete the [EDW Basic Form](https://oklahoma.gov/content/dam/ok/en/elections/ok-election-data-warehouse/edw-form-basic.pdf)
3. Complete the [Voter Registration List Form](https://oklahoma.gov/content/dam/ok/en/elections/ok-election-data-warehouse/edw-form-vrlist.pdf)
4. Submit forms to register for portal access
5. Once approved, access data through the online portal

### What's Included

The voter registration file includes comprehensive information for all registered voters in Oklahoma.

## Voter History

Oklahoma includes voter history data with the voter registration list for approved users.

### Historical Coverage

Voter history records are available through the same portal access, providing election participation data for registered voters.

## Cost

Oklahoma's voter data is **completely free** for qualified requestors. There is no charge for access to the data warehouse.

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

Access is limited to:
- Oklahoma residents who are U.S. citizens
- Official party representatives
- Candidates and their representatives
- Other persons authorized by state law

When accessing Oklahoma voter data, users must comply with state laws regarding the use of voter registration records.

## Technical Notes

### Portal Access

Once approved, users gain access to the Oklahoma Election Data Warehouse portal for downloading voter data.

### Testing Status

This system has not been independently tested as it requires Oklahoma residency or other qualifying criteria. However, the state's portal-based system appears well-designed for qualified users.

---

Oklahoma's free voter data portal offers an excellent resource for qualified requestors within the state. While access is restricted to eligible users, those who qualify benefit from no-cost access to comprehensive voter registration and history data.
