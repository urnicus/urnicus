Pennsylvania offers one of the most accessible and affordable voter data systems in the United States. Both voter registration lists and voter history records can be obtained quickly through the state's online portal at a minimal cost.

## Quick Summary

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Statewide Cost** | $20 (covers all 67 counties) |
| **Voter List Access** | Available online via state portal |
| **Voter History Access** | Included with voter list (last 40 elections) |
| **Delivery Method** | Immediate download via email link |
| **Processing Time** | Minutes after payment |

## Voter List

Pennsylvania provides voter registration data through the Department of State's online voter services portal. You can request data at the county level or for the entire state.

### How to Access

1. Visit the [PA Full Voter Export purchase page](https://www.pavoterservices.pa.gov/pages/PurchasePAFullVoterExport.aspx?Langcode=en-US)
2. Select the county or counties you want (or select all 67 counties for statewide data)
3. Complete the online purchase form
4. Pay the required fee
5. Receive a download link via email within minutes

### What's Included

The Full Voter Export file includes comprehensive voter registration information for all registered voters in the selected jurisdiction(s).

## Voter History

Pennsylvania includes voter history data directly in the Full Voter Export file, making it unnecessary to submit a separate request.

### Historical Coverage

The voter history file contains participation records for the **last 40 elections**, providing extensive historical voting behavior data. This is one of the most comprehensive voter history datasets available among U.S. states.

## Cost

The entire statewide voter list with voter history costs just **$20**.

You can purchase data for individual counties at the same rate, or select all counties for the flat $20 fee. This makes Pennsylvania one of the most affordable states for obtaining comprehensive voter data.

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

When requesting Pennsylvania voter data, you must affirm compliance with state law regarding the use of voter registration records.

> **Important Legal Requirements:**
>
> "I affirm that any information obtained from the records requested from the Department of State will not be used for commercial or other purposes, except purposes related to elections, political activities and law enforcement, as required by 25 Pa.C.S. sections 1207(b) & 1404(c)(2). I further affirm that I will not publish any of the above lists on the Internet, as such publication is prohibited by 4 Pa. Code sections 183.13 (g) & 183.14 (k)."

### Permitted Uses

- Elections-related activities
- Political activities
- Law enforcement purposes

### Prohibited Uses

- Commercial solicitation
- Publishing voter lists on the Internet
- Any purpose not related to elections, political activities, or law enforcement

## Technical Notes

### Browser Compatibility

The Pennsylvania Department of State has noted browser compatibility issues with their download system:

> "Notice: The download of the PA Full Voter Export is not currently compatible with Google Chrome. For best results, we suggest using Internet Explorer. Mozilla Firefox can also download the files, but the Read Me and Political Party files are sometimes downloaded without their file extensions (.doc and .txt, respectively)."

**Note:** Despite this official notice, recent testing (November 2025) indicates the download system works correctly with modern browsers, including Chrome.

## Additional Resources

For detailed information about data formats and field definitions, consult the [Election Data Handbook](https://www.pa.gov/content/dam/copapwp-pagov/en/dos/resources/voting-and-elections/election%20data%20handbook%20general.pdf) published by the Pennsylvania Department of State.
