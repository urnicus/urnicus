## Voter List

Delaware provides voter registration data through the State Election Commissioner's office with data fields varying based on the requestor's classification.

### How to Access

1. Visit the [Delaware Elections Purchase Reports page](https://elections.delaware.gov/candidates/purchasereports.shtml)
2. Download the [Statewide Voter Registration Order Form PDF](https://elections.delaware.gov/public/forms/pdfs/statewide_vreg_order_form.pdf)
3. Complete the order form
4. Mail the form with payment ($25) to the address specified
5. Receive the file via secure email after processing

### What's Included

The voter registration file content varies based on requestor type:

**For General Public:**
- Voter names
- Addresses
- Political party affiliations
- Voting history
- Legislative district information
- Years of birth

**For Candidates for Elective Office and Major/Minor Political Parties:**
- All public fields, plus:
- Telephone numbers
- Full dates of birth (year only for public)

**For Members of General Assembly and State/County/Local Government Agencies:**
- All candidate fields, plus:
- Full dates of birth
- Additional information as authorized

## Voter History

Delaware includes voter history data with the voter registration list for all requestor types.

### Historical Coverage

Voting history is included in all voter registration files. Contact the State Election Commissioner for details about the historical coverage period.

## Cost

Delaware's voter data costs just **$25** for the statewide list with voter history, making it one of the most affordable states for comprehensive voter data.

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

When requesting Delaware voter data, users must comply with state laws regarding the use of voter registration records. The order form will include specific terms of use and legal requirements.

Different data fields are provided based on requestor type, as specified in Delaware Code, Title 15, Chapter 3, ยง304(h).

## Technical Notes

### Delivery Method

Files are sent via **secure email** after the order form and payment are received and processed.

### Requestor Classification

Carefully review the order form to properly identify your requestor type, as this determines which data fields you will receive:
1. General public
2. Candidates for elective office
3. Major and minor political parties
4. Members of General Assembly
5. State agencies or county/local governments
