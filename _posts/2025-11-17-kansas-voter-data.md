## Voter List

Kansas provides voter registration data through the Secretary of State's office under KORA (Kansas Open Records Act) provisions.

### How to Access

1. Review the [KORA Policy Statement](https://www.sos.ks.gov/general-services/kora-policy-statement.html) for general information
2. Download the [Computerized Voter Registration Form (CVR)](https://sos.ks.gov/forms/Elections/CVR.pdf)
3. Complete the CVR form
4. Mail the completed form with payment ($200) to the address specified on the form
5. Receive voter data after processing

### What's Included

The computerized voter registration file includes information for registered voters in Kansas.

## Voter History

The availability of voter history data is not explicitly spelled out in the available documentation.

### Recommendation

When submitting your CVR form, specifically ask about:
- Whether voter history is included with the voter registration file
- Whether voter history is available as a separate file
- What historical coverage is provided
- Any additional cost for voter history

It's recommended to **call the Secretary of State's office** to confirm voter history availability before submitting your request.
