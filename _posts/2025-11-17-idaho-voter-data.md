Idaho provides access to voter registration data and voter history through an online request form at a minimal cost of $20. The state delivers data via email as CSV files, making it one of the most affordable and accessible voter data systems in the United States.

## Quick Summary

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Statewide Cost** | $20 |
| **Voter List Access** | Online request form |
| **Voter History Access** | Selectable option on form |
| **Delivery Method** | Email (CSV files) |
| **Processing Time** | After form submission and payment |

## Voter List

Idaho provides voter registration data through the Secretary of State's online public records request system.

### How to Access

1. Visit the [Idaho Voter Registration Request Form](https://forms.sos.idaho.gov/voter-registration-request-form/?_gl=1*1s3g55f*_ga*MTkxODgwMjU1Ni4xNzYzMTMyMzcx*_ga_SN820FRG5P*czE3NjMxMzIzNzAkbzEkZzAkdDE3NjMxMzIzNzAkajYwJGwwJGgw)
2. Complete the online form
3. Select the data you need (statewide or specific jurisdiction)
4. Submit payment ($20)
5. Receive CSV files via email

Additional information:
- [Public Records Request Information](https://sos.idaho.gov/public-records-requests/)
- [Absentee/Voter Registration Report Details PDF](https://sos.idaho.gov/downloads/PRR_Absentee_VoterReg_Report.pdf)

### What's Included

The statewide voter registration list includes information for all registered voters in Idaho.

## Voter History

Idaho provides voter history as a selectable option on the voter registration request form.

### How to Access

When completing the voter registration request form, check the option to include voter history data. This will be included in the CSV files sent via email.

### Historical Coverage

Contact the Secretary of State for details about the historical coverage period included in the voter history file.

## Cost

Idaho charges just **$20** for statewide voter registration data with voter history, making it one of the most affordable states for comprehensive voter data.

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

> **Important Legal Requirements:**
>
> Pursuant to Section 34-437A(3), Idaho Code:
>
> "No person to whom a list of statewide electors is furnished and no person who acquires a list of statewide electors prepared from such list shall use any information contained therein for the purpose of mailing or delivering any advertisement or offer for any property, establishment, organization, product, or service or for the purpose of mailing or delivering any solicitation for money, services, or anything of value. Provided however, that any such list and label may be used for any political purpose."

### Permitted Uses
- Political purposes

### Prohibited Uses
- Commercial advertisements or offers
- Solicitations for money, services, or anything of value
- Any non-political commercial use

## Technical Notes

### Delivery Method

Data is sent via **email as CSV files** after the request is processed and payment is received.

### File Format

Idaho provides voter data in **CSV (comma-separated values)** format, which is easily imported into spreadsheets, databases, and data analysis tools.

### Online Request System

The online form streamlines the request process, making it quick and straightforward to:
- Specify exactly what data you need
- Include voter history with a checkbox
- Submit payment electronically
- Receive files via email

### Testing Status

This system has not been independently tested, but the online form and email delivery system appear to be low-effort and user-friendly. Contact the Secretary of State if you have questions about the process.
