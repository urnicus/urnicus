## Voter List

Oregon provides voter registration data through the Secretary of State's Elections Division with an online request system.

### How to Access

1. Visit the [Oregon Request for Voter List page](https://sos.oregon.gov/elections/Pages/request-for-voter-list.aspx)
2. Complete the online request form
3. Submit the form for review
4. Complete payment ($500)
5. Receive voter data after processing

### What's Included

The voter registration file includes comprehensive information for all registered voters in Oregon.

## Voter History

Oregon includes voter history data with the voter registration list.

### Historical Coverage

Voter history records are provided along with the voter registration data. Contact the Secretary of State for details about the historical coverage period included in the voter history file.

## Cost

Oregon charges **$500** for statewide voter registration data with voter history.

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

When requesting Oregon voter data, users must comply with state laws regarding the use of voter registration records. The online request form will include specific terms of use and legal requirements that must be acknowledged during the request process.
