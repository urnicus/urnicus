Montana provides access to voter registration data and voter history through an online purchase portal. The state charges $1,000 for statewide voter data with voter history available as a selectable option.

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Statewide Cost** | $1,000 |

| **Delivery Method** | Online download |
| **Processing Time** | After payment processed |

## Voter List

Montana provides voter registration data through an online voter file purchase portal managed by the Secretary of State.

### How to Access

1. Visit the [Montana Purchase Voter File page](https://prodvoterportal.mt.gov/PurchaseVoterFile.aspx)
2. Complete the online purchase form
3. Select statewide data or specific jurisdictions
4. Add voter history if desired (selectable option)
5. Complete payment ($1,000 for statewide)
6. Download voter data after processing

### What's Included

The voter file includes comprehensive voter registration information for registered voters in Montana.

## Voter History

Montana offers voter history as a selectable option during the purchase process on the online portal.

### How to Access

When purchasing voter data through the portal, check the option to include voter history data with your purchase.

### Historical Coverage

Review the portal or contact the Secretary of State for details about the historical coverage period included in the voter history file.

## Cost

Montana charges **$1,000** for statewide voter registration data. Voter history is available as an additional selectable option (pricing may vary based on selection).

## Restrictions and Permitted Uses

When purchasing Montana voter data, users must comply with state laws regarding the use of voter registration records. The online portal will include specific terms of use and legal requirements during the purchase process.
