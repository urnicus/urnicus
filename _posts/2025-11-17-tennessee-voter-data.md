Tennessee provides access to voter registration data through the State Election Commission with a maximum cost of $2,500 for the statewide list. Unlike many states, Tennessee does not offer an online purchase option, requiring direct contact with the Division of Elections.

## Voter List

Tennessee provides voter registration data through the Tennessee Secretary of State - Division of Elections, but does not offer an online purchase system.

### How to Access

1. **Contact the Division of Elections before proceeding:**
   - **Phone**: 615-741-7956 or 1-877-850-4959
   - **Email**: tennessee.elections@tn.gov
   - **Address**: 312 Rosa L. Parks Avenue, Snodgrass Tower, 7th Floor, Nashville, TN 37243

2. Inquire about current process for obtaining voter registration data

3. Available documentation (currency uncertain):
   - [State CD Purchase Information](https://www.votesumnertn.org/documents/candidate_info/state-cd-purchase.pdf)
   - This document appears to be dated and may not reflect current procedures

4. Request voter list and any required forms/affidavits

5. Submit payment when instructed

6. Receive voter data via method specified by Division of Elections

### What's Included

The voter registration file includes information for registered voters in Tennessee.

## Voter History

Voter history can be requested when submitting your affidavit or request for voter registration data.

### How to Request

When contacting the Division of Elections:
- Specify that you want voter history included
- Ask about historical coverage period
- Verify any additional costs for voter history
- Confirm file format and structure

## Cost

Tennessee charges up to **$2,500** for the statewide voter registration list.

This maximum cap provides cost certainty for statewide data requests.

## Technical Notes

### No Online Purchase

Unlike many states, Tennessee does not offer:
- Online purchase portal
- Self-service data requests
- Automated payment processing
- Immediate downloads

The contact-based system requires direct communication with the Division of Elections.

### Affidavit Requirement

Based on available documentation, Tennessee likely requires an affidavit or formal request form. Confirm current requirements when contacting the Division of Elections.

### CD Delivery

Historical documentation references CD delivery, though current delivery methods may include:
- Physical media (CD, flash drive)

Verify current delivery methods with the Division of Elections.
