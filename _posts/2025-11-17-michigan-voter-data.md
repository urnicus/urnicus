## Voter List

Michigan provides voter registration data through Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests submitted to the Secretary of State's office.

### How to Access

1. Download the [Michigan FOIA Form PDF](https://www.michigan.gov/-/media/Project/Websites/sos/02lehman/FOIA_FORM.pdf?rev=b1ac9e66e4fd4d97ace0e4d9194ceb64)
2. Complete the FOIA request form
3. Specify statewide voter registration data
4. Check the voter history option if desired
5. Select delivery method (flash drive or FTP)
6. Submit the form via email to the address specified
7. Pay applicable FOIA fees when invoiced
8. Receive voter data via your selected delivery method

### What's Included

The voter registration file includes comprehensive information for all registered voters in Michigan.

## Voter History

Michigan offers voter history as a selectable option on the FOIA request form.

### How to Access

When completing the FOIA form, check the box or option to include voter history data with your voter registration request.

### Historical Coverage

Contact the Secretary of State or review the FOIA form for details about the historical coverage period included in the voter history file.

## Cost

Michigan's voter data is exceptionally affordable through the FOIA process, with estimates ranging from **$23 to $50** for statewide data.

This low cost is based on Michigan's FOIA guidelines, which limit fees to:
- Actual cost of copying/duplication
- Media costs (flash drive, etc.)
- Limited labor charges
- No profit or excessive fees

### Cost Components

Under Michigan FOIA law, agencies may charge for:
- Cost of media (flash drive if selected)
- Actual duplication costs
- Limited staff time (subject to statutory limits)
- FTP setup if applicable (usually minimal or free)

The $23-$50 estimate reflects these modest allowable costs.

## Delivery Methods

Michigan offers two flexible delivery options:

**1. Flash Drive**
- Data provided on physical USB flash drive
- Mailed to your address
- Small media cost included in total fee
- No special technical setup required

**2. FTP (File Transfer Protocol)**
- Digital delivery via FTP server
- Faster than physical media
- May require you to provide FTP server credentials
- Minimal or no additional cost

Choose the delivery method that best suits your technical capabilities and timeline.

Under Michigan FOIA law:

- Large requests may take longer
